# Summary: Selection vs Compression

## 1. Sparse Truth -> Lasso is more natural
Lasso directly selects original variables, matching the data generation.

## 2. Latent-factor Truth -> PCR is more natural
PCR aggregates information via principal components.

## 3. Lasso answers 'which variables matter', PCR answers 'how to compress information'

## 4. Business wants short variable list -> Lasso
## 5. Business wants stable prediction -> Ridge / PCR
